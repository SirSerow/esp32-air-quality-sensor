Temperature AHT20

Ambient air temperature measured by the AHT20 capacitive sensor on the I²C bus. Reflects how warm or cool the air is — high temperatures reduce comfort and increase CO₂ generation from people.

LevelRangeMeaning
Good≤ 26 °CComfortable indoors
Moderate26 – 30 °CWarm, may affect comfort
High> 30 °CHot, ventilate or cool down
Typical indoor comfort: 18 – 24 °C
Relative Humidity AHT20

Percentage of water vapour in the air relative to saturation. Too low causes dry air and static; too high promotes mould, dust mites, and condensation.

LevelRangeMeaning
Good30 – 60 %Comfortable, healthy
Moderate60 – 70 %Humid, monitor for mould
High> 70 % or < 20 %Too humid or too dry
Ideal: 40 – 55 % for most people
eCO₂ ENS160

Equivalent CO₂ concentration in ppm, algorithmically estimated from TVOC readings. Not a direct CO₂ measurement — it correlates with human activity and air freshness. High values mean stale air.

LevelRangeMeaning
Fresh< 800 ppmGood air quality
Moderate800 – 2 000 ppmVentilate the room
Poor> 2 000 ppmOpen windows immediately
Outdoor baseline ≈ 400 ppm · Unsafe > 5 000 ppm
Air Quality Index ENS160

Proprietary ENS160 index from 1 (excellent) to 5 (unhealthy). Combines TVOC, humidity-compensation, and gas-sensor data into a single score. Useful as a quick at-a-glance indicator.

ScoreLabelAction
1 – 2Excellent / GoodNo action needed
3ModerateMay affect sensitive people
4 – 5Poor / UnhealthyVentilate immediately
Readings during warm-up (validity ≠ 0) may be unreliable
TVOC ENS160

Total Volatile Organic Compounds in ppb. VOCs include gases from cleaning products, furniture, paints, cooking, and human breath. Chronic exposure to elevated TVOC can affect health.

LevelRangeMeaning
Good< 220 ppbClean air
Moderate220 – 660 ppbSome VOC sources present
High> 660 ppbIdentify and remove sources
Common sources: sprays, candles, new furniture, cooking
ENS Validity ENS160

Internal status flag of the ENS160 chip indicating whether the sensor output is reliable. Air quality readings during states 1–3 should be treated with caution.

ValueStateMeaning
0NormalReadings are valid
1Warm-upFirst ~30 s after power-on
2Start-upInitial conditioning (~3 min)
3InvalidSensor error, check wiring
Always check validity before acting on air-quality readings
